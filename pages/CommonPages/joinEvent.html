About This Event

Join us for a morning of beach cleaning and environmental awareness. Help protect our oceans and marine life by removing plastic waste and debris from Batu Ferringhi Beach. This is a great opportunity to meet like-minded individuals who care about the environment while making a real difference in our local community.

We will provide all necessary equipment including gloves, trash bags, and recycling bins. Please wear comfortable clothing and bring your own water bottle. Refreshments will be provided after the clean-up activity.

All ages are welcome! Families with children are encouraged to participate. This is a perfect activity to teach the next generation about environmental stewardship and the importance of keeping our beaches clean.
